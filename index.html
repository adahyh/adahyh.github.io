What I've been up to...
✦ Research Assistant @ YourMove Research Study
My passion for healthcare and design led me to the YourMove Research study. As a research assistant, I analyze participant interviews and transcripts, uncovering themes and motivations behind their behaviors. This intersection of design and healthcare illustrates how thoughtful design choices can influence behavior and make a real impact on people's health and well-being!
✦ Interaction Design Member @ Menolearn, UCSD Global TIES
My team and I partnered with the non-profit My MenoPlan to create an emphathetic AI chatbot and resource for women going through menopause, called Menolearn. My role included analyzing interviews of doctors and menopausal women to determine what resources should be included in addition to the chatbot, creating user personas, and designing a high-fidelity prototype of the website.
✦ UIUX Design Member, Team Creative Director @ A Happier Me
For a class project, my team and I partnered with the wellbeing initiative A Happier Me. We strove to create a mindfulness journal app, emphasizing brand identity and creating something new but consistent with the organization's other products.
✦ Apprentice @ Visual Design Apprenticeship, Design Co
Interested in honing my design skills, I joined the Visual Design Apprenticeship and created 5 projects under the mentoring of 7 designers covering the topics of visual, graphic, and brand design. My favorite project was creating a rebrand of Happy Lemon, a beverage company, including designing a new mascot.
